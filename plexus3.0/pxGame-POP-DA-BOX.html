Pop the Box

How to Play:

  1. Click "Start" to begin the challenge!
  2. Buttons with numbers will pop up.
  3. Your mission: Pop buttons to reach 55.
  4. Beat the timer, or you'll lose the round!
  5. First to win 10 times is the ultimate champion!
  6. Pop all buttons before resetting for the next round.
  7. Stay below 55 to keep the game alive!
  8. Reset and start for the next thrilling round.

You have seconds to click buttons.
